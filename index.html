Phone setup

  1. Open the regular Spotify app on your phone and start any song once.
  2. Open this walk-up app and tap Refresh Device.
  3. Choose your phone in the Spotify device list, then tap Use This Phone.
  4. Tap a player to play just that clip on your active Spotify device.

This version controls the Spotify app on your phone instead of trying to play audio inside the mobile browser.
